Description

Blogit was first conceived during my undergrad days in year 2011. It was during that time when I got my first Android device and I realized this blogger application wasn't available in the Google Play store. Finding it somewhat cumbersome to have to login to my blogger account via the mobile browser, I finally decided to work on a mobile app which makes use of the Blogger API for easy blogging.
